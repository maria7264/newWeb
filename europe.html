Adidas Superstar

Italy: The Adidas Superstar made waves as the first low-cut, leather basketball sneaker. It also earned the nickname "Shell Toe" because of the shape of the shoe's toe area.[1]

Nike Air Max 95

France: Since its debut, the Air Max 95 has been a phenomenon around the globe, seemingly gaining more fans each year. Originally designed by Nike’s Sergio Lozano, the Air Max 95 is most famous in its original “Neon” colorway, which features a gradient colorblock of thin, wavy panels that go from white at the mesh lining near the laces to black at the midsole. Neon green details add contrast against the neutral tones, offering standout style. The “Neon” look is certainly the most famous, but the Air Max 95 has dropped in countless colorways and materials in the years since, including many styles exclusive to kids' sizes.
